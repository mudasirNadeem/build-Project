Why do I need an billing software?

If you own a business that sells something, then you need to issue bill/invoice to your clients, manage inventory and keep an tab on your sales and accounts to know how much money you're making each month. You can do all this using our billing software and tightens your grip over business and look more professional to your clients.

How can I purchase billing software license?

Simply go to our pricing page, select the business edition, fill out purchase form and you'll be redirected to payment gateway where you can pay using your debit/credit card and net banking. After successful payment completion you'll receive a confirmation email with your license keys. Alternatively you can contact our resellers for offline purchase.

Does your billing software supports GST?

Since GST notified by Government of India our billing software is fully GST compliant. Hitech BillSoft supports all GST features like GSTIN, HSN/SAC Code, CGST, SGST, IGST and GSTR reports like GSTR-1, GSTR-3B. You can mail GSTR reports directly from application to your auditor.
